    Maritz Lab at HAA

    Bryan, Jody, JJ, and Jannie headed to Zululand in January for the HAA meeting. Jody and JJ gave their first conference talks and both were well-received. Bryan was on the organising committee and chaired several sessions during the meeting. Overall, a very smooth and enjoyable conference. As always, the meeting was rich with ideas and old friendships.
